2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for several purposes:

  • For Communication: To send notifications, updates, or respond to inquiries.
  • For Improvements: To analyze usage trends and improve our platform, adding new features or enhancing existing ones.
  • To Process Payments: To manage transactions between farmers and dairy owners.
  • For Legal Compliance: To comply with legal obligations, such as tax and regulatory requirements.

  • With Your Consent: We may share your data with third parties when you explicitly provide consent.

4. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, or as required by law. Once data is no longer required, we will securely delete or anonymize it.
